The world is run by five ruling kingdoms: [[The Ancient People]], [[The Iron Empire]], [[The Sentinels]], [[The Sky Kingdom]], and [[The Tribes]]. The kingdoms are separated by species. In this campaign, party's reputation with each kingdom will be tracked and players will be able to rank up within each kingdom to gain various benefits. The campaign will start in the [[City of Peace]] on the [[Isle of Tranquility]].
