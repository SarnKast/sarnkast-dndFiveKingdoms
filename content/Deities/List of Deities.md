The gods, or deities, of this world are split into two categories: The Prime Deities, mostly of neutral and good alignments, and The Betrayer Gods, consisting of the evil aligned gods.

Prime Deities:
- [[Avandra]], the Changebringer
- [[Bahamut]], the Platinum Dragon
- [[Corellon]], the Arch Heart
- [[Erathis]], the Lawbearer
- [[Iuon]], the Knowing Mistress
- [[Kord]], the Stormlord
- [[Melora]], the Wildmother
- [[Moradin]], the All-Hammer
- [[Pelor]], the Dawnfather
- [[The Raven Queen]], the Matron of Ravens
- [[Sarenrae]], the Everlight
- [[Sahanine]], the Moonweaver

Betrayer Gods:
- [[Asmodeus]], the Lord of the Nine Hells
- [[Bane]], the Strife Emperor
- [[Gruumsh]], the Ruiner
- [[Lolth]], the Spider Queen
- [[Tharizdun]], the Chained Oblivion
- [[Tiamat]], the Scaled Tyrant
- [[Torog]], the Crawling King
- [[Vecna]], the Whispered One
- [[Zehir]], the Cloaked Serpent
