Made up of the [[Goliaths]] and the [[Orcs]]. The wandering tribes of the Goliaths and the Orcs are mostly a nomadic people, and are known to be great hunters. They are in charge of defending the [[City of Peace]] from the monsters within the [[Forest of Anger]]. They also run the city's apothecaries.
